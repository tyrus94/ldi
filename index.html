Our Journey

Crafting exceptional spaces since 2015

2015

Studio Founding

LDinteriors was established with a vision to transform living spaces into personalized sanctuaries that reflect the unique essence of each client.

2017

First Commercial Project

Completed our first major commercial design for The Artisan Café, setting the foundation for our distinctive approach to hospitality spaces.

2019

Design Excellence Award

Honored with the East African Design Excellence Award for our innovative approach to sustainable luxury in residential design.

2021

Studio Expansion

Expanded our team and opened a larger studio in Westlands to accommodate our growing client base and enhance our design capabilities.

2023

International Projects

Broadened our horizons with our first international projects in Tanzania and Uganda, bringing our distinctive East African design sensibility to new markets.

2025

The Future

Continuing to push boundaries in sustainable design while embracing new technologies that enhance both aesthetics and functionality in living spaces.
